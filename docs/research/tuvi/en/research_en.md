Tứ Hóa System (Four Transformations)\n\n### 3.1 Complete Tứ Hóa Table by Thiên Can of Birth Year\n\n| Thiên Can | Hóa Lộc | Hóa Quyền | Hóa Khoa | Hóa Kỵ |\n|-----------|---------|----------|---------|---------|\n| Giáp | Liêm Trinh | Phá Quân | Vũ Khúc | Thái Dương |\n| Ất | Thiên Cơ | Thiên Lương | Tử Vi | Thái Âm |\n| Bính | Thiên Đồng | Thiên Cơ | Văn Xương | Liêm Trinh |\n| Đinh | Thái Âm | Thiên Đồng | Thiên Cơ | Cự Môn |\n| Mậu | Tham Lang | Thái Âm | Hữu Bật | Thiên Cơ |\n| Kỷ | Vũ Khúc | Tham Lang | Thiên Lương | Văn Khúc |\n| Canh | Thái Dương | Vũ Khúc | Thái Âm | Thiên Đồng |\n| Tân | Cự Môn | Thái Dương | Văn Khúc | Văn Xương |\n| Nhâm | Thiên Lương | Tử Vi | Tả Phụ | Vũ Khúc |\n| Quý | Phá Quân | Cự Môn | Thái Âm | Tham Lang |\n\n**Meaning of each Hóa:**\n- **Hóa Lộc:** Wealth, income, prosperity — the \"Fortune\" transformation\n- **Hóa Quyền:** Power, authority, control — the \"Power\" transformation\n- **Hóa Khoa:** Fame, reputation, academic success — the \"Glory\" transformation\n- **Hóa Kỵ:** Obstacles, blockages, difficulties — the \"Taboo\" transformation\n\n### 3.2 Phi Hóa (Flying Transformation)\n\nPhi Hóa is an advanced technique used primarily in the Khâm Thiên and Phi Tinh Tứ Hóa schools. The mechanism:\n\n1. Each palace has its own Thiên Can (the can chi of the palace, see Section 4)\n2. From that palace's can, look up the Tứ Hóa table\n3. Project the resulting hóa stars from that palace into other palaces\n\nExample: \"Cung Mệnh Phi Hóa Lộc nhập Cung Tài Bạch\" (Destiny palace's can causes Hóa Lộc to fly into the Wealth palace) = wealth accumulation is a core life theme.\n\n**Standard Tứ Hóa** (birth year can) marks innate destiny patterns.\n**Phi Hóa** (each palace's can) shows dynamic cause-and-effect relationships between palaces.\n\nThis is what TinhMenhDo calls \"Khâm Thiên Tứ Hóa\" — the most advanced reading layer.\n\n### 3.3 Mệnh Chủ and Thân Chủ\n\n**Mệnh Chủ** — determined by the earthly branch of the Cung Mệnh (destiny palace):\n\n| Cung Mệnh at | Mệnh Chủ Star |\n|-------------|--------------|\n| Tý | Tham Lang |\n| Sửu | Cự Môn |\n| Dần | Lộc Tồn |\n| Mão | Văn Khúc |\n| Thìn | Liêm Trinh |\n| Tỵ | Vũ Khúc |\n| Ngọ | Phá Quân |\n| Mùi | Vũ Khúc |\n| Thân | Liêm Trinh |\n| Dậu | Văn Khúc |\n| Tuất | Lộc Tồn |\n| Hợi | Cự Môn |\n\n**Thân Chủ** — determined by the earthly branch of the birth year:\n\n| Birth Year Branch | Thân Chủ Star |\n|------------------|--------------|\n| Tý | Hỏa Tinh |\n| Sửu | Thiên Tướng |\n| Dần | Thiên Lương |\n| Mão | Thiên Đồng |\n| Thìn | Văn Xương |\n| Tỵ | Thiên Cơ |\n| Ngọ | Linh Tinh |\n| Mùi | Thiên Tướng |\n| Thân | Thiên Lương |\n| Dậu | Thiên Đồng |\n| Tuất | Văn Xương |\n| Hợi | Thiên Cơ |\n\n---\n\n## 4. Palace (Cung) Details\n\n### 4.1 The 12 Palaces in Order\n\nStarting from Cung Mệnh and going counter-clockwise (the standard Vietnamese layout):\n\n| # | Palace Name | Life Domain |\n|---|------------|-------------|\n| 1 | Mệnh | Destiny, personality, life overview |\n| 2 | Phụ Mẫu | Parents, superiors, documents |\n| 3 | Phúc Đức | Blessings, inner life, spirituality |\n| 4 | Điền Trạch | Property, real estate, home |\n| 5 | Quan Lộc | Career, achievement, reputation |\n| 6 | Nô Bộc | Subordinates, employees, friends |\n| 7 | Thiên Di | Travel, overseas, change |\n| 8 | Tật Ách | Health, illness, hidden obstacles |\n| 9 | Tài Bạch | Wealth, income, finances |\n| 10 | Tử Tức | Children, creative output |\n| 11 | Phu Thê | Spouse, relationships |\n| 12 | Huynh Đệ | Siblings, peers |\n\nThe **Thân Cung** (Body Palace) is a separate palace identified by the birth hour — it co-exists with one of the 12 palaces above and represents the physical body and later-life fortune (after age 35-40).\n\n### 4.2 Can Chi of Each Palace\n\nEach palace has both an earthly branch (fixed) and a heavenly stem (derived from birth year's can). The 12 palaces always occupy fixed earthly branches in the 12-cell grid. The heavenly stems cycle through based on the formula:\n\nThe heavenly stem of Cung Mệnh is derived from the birth year's heavenly stem and birth month, using the \"Ngũ Hổ Độn\" rule:\n\n| Birth Year's Can | Stem of Tháng Giêng (1st Lunar Month, Dần) |\n|-----------------|------------------------------------------|\n| Giáp or Kỷ | Bính |\n| Ất or Canh | Mậu |\n| Bính or Tân | Canh |\n| Đinh or Nhâm | Nhâm |\n| Mậu or Quý | Giáp |\n\nFrom the first month's stem, count forward through the stems to find each subsequent month's stem. The palace whose earthly branch matches the birth month's earthly branch gets that month's heavenly stem. All other palaces' stems follow the 60-year sexagenary sequence: e.g., Dần gets Bính → Mão gets Đinh → Thìn gets Mậu... continuing through the 10 stems cyclically.\n\nThis is why a professional chart shows each palace labeled like \"Ất Mão\" or \"Bính Thìn\" — the stem-branch combination is the can chi of that palace, used for Phi Hóa calculations.\n\n### 4.3 Palace Scoring System (Điểm Cung)\n\nThe TinhMenhDo scoring system (values like 102, 92, 82, etc.) is a proprietary algorithm not publicly documented in traditional texts. Based on observable patterns, it likely works by:\n\n1. Counting the brightness scores of all stars in the palace:\n   - Miếu star: +4 points\n   - Vượng star: +3 points\n   - Đắc địa: +2 points\n   - Bình hòa: +1 point\n   - Hãm địa: -2 to -4 points\n2. Adding bonus points for auspicious stars (Hóa Lộc, Tả Phụ, Hữu Bật, etc.)\n3. Subtracting for malefic stars (Hóa Kỵ, Kình Dương, Hỏa Linh, etc.)\n4. Normalizing to a 0–120 scale where 102 = excellent, 82 = below average, etc.\n\nThis is not a traditional Tử Vi concept — it is TinhMenhDo's own gamification layer to help users quickly compare palace strengths.\n\n---\n\n## 5. Đại Hạn (Major Fortune Periods)\n\n### 5.1 Cục Types and Starting Ages\n\nThe Cục (elemental classification) determines where the first Đại Hạn starts:\n\n| Cục | Element | Number | Starting Age of 1st Đại Hạn |\n|-----|---------|--------|---------------------------|\n| Thủy Nhị Cục | Water | 2 | Age 2 (period: 2–11) |\n| Mộc Tam Cục | Wood | 3 | Age 3 (period: 3–12) |\n| Kim Tứ Cục | Metal | 4 | Age 4 (period: 4–13) |\n| Thổ Ngũ Cục | Earth | 5 | Age 5 (period: 5–14) |\n| Hỏa Lục Cục | Fire | 6 | Age 6 (period: 6–15) |\n\nEach Đại Hạn covers 10 years. The first period starts at Cung Mệnh and moves to the next palace every 10 years.\n\n### 5.2 Đại Hạn Direction Rules\n\n| Person Type | Direction |\n|-------------|-----------|\n| Dương Nam (Yang Male — born in Giáp, Bính, Mậu, Canh, Nhâm years) | Thuận (clockwise / forward) |\n| Âm Nữ (Yin Female — born in Ất, Đinh, Kỷ, Tân, Quý years) | Thuận (clockwise / forward) |\n| Âm Nam (Yin Male) | Nghịch (counter-clockwise / reverse) |\n| Dương Nữ (Yang Female) | Nghịch (counter-clockwise / reverse) |\n\n**Display:** Each palace shows the age range of its Đại Hạn period in the corner (e.g., \"2-11\", \"12-21\"). These are **tuổi ta** (Vietnamese age = Western age + 1, since Vietnamese count age from 1 at birth). Many modern apps show both tuổi ta and tuổi tây (Western age).\n\n---\n\n## 6. Tiểu Hạn and Lưu Niên\n\n### 6.1 Tiểu Hạn (Annual Fortune Cycle)\n\nTiểu Hạn moves through the 12 palaces, spending exactly 1 year in each palace. The 12-year cycle then repeats.\n\n**Starting palace:** Determined by the person's birth year earthly branch group (tam hợp):\n\n| Birth Year Tri-Harmony Group | Tiểu Hạn Starts at (Age 1) |\n|-----------------------------|-----------------------------|\n| Hợi, Mão, Mùi (Mộc cục group) | Sửu palace |\n| Dần, Ngọ, Tuất (Hỏa cục group) | Thìn palace |\n| Thân, Tý, Thìn (Thủy cục group) | Mùi palace |\n| Tỵ, Dậu, Sửu (Kim cục group) | Tuất palace |\n\n**Direction:** Nam (males) move thuận (forward/clockwise); Nữ (females) move nghịch (backward/counter-clockwise).\n\n**Interpretation:** The palace Tiểu Hạn occupies in any given year becomes the \"Mệnh\" of that year — read it with its stars, transformations, and the visiting Vòng Thái Tuế stars for that year's fortune.\n\n### 6.2 Lưu Niên (Annual Year Overlay)\n\nLưu Niên means overlaying the current year's energy onto the chart. Each year, you identify:\n\n1. **Lưu Niên Cung Mệnh:** The palace whose earthly branch matches the current year's earthly branch — this becomes the \"Mệnh\" for that year\n2. **Vòng Thái Tuế:** The 12 Thái Tuế stars placed starting from the current year's earthly branch (Thái Tuế itself always at the current year's branch)\n3. **Lưu Niên Tứ Hóa:** The Tứ Hóa derived from the current year's heavenly stem — these transformation stars float over the natal chart for that year\n4. **Lưu Niên Lộc Tồn:** Lộc Tồn placed at the current year's stem's corresponding branch\n\n**Professional app display:** Most apps (TinhMenhDo, Lichvannien) allow the user to select a year and then overlay these annual elements on top of the natal chart, often using a different color (e.g., red or gold) to distinguish them from natal stars.\n\n**Lưu Niên vs Tiểu Hạn:** Tiểu Hạn = \"the flower and fruit\" (visible outcome of that year); Lưu Niên = \"the seed and soil\" (the underlying potential/cause). Professional interpretation always reads both together.\n\n---\n\n## 7.
